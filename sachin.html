Sachin Tendulkar is a retired Indian cricketer widely regarded as one of the greatest batsmen in the history of cricket. He was born on April 24, 1973, in Mumbai, India. He is the only player to have scored 100 international centuries and holds numerous other records in both Test and One Day International (ODI) cricket.
Tendulkar's passion for cricket began at a young age. He made his debut for India at the age of 16. He played for Mumbai in domestic cricket and the Mumbai Indians in the Indian Premier League (IPL). Achievements and Records: Highest Run-Scorer: He holds the record for the most runs scored in both Test and ODI cricket. 100 International Centuries: He is the only player to have achieved this milestone. First to Score a Double Century in ODIs: He was the first to reach this landmark in One Day Internationals. Other Notable Records: He has numerous other records in both Test and ODI cricket. Personal Life: Tendulkar married Anjali Mehta in 1995. They have two children, Sara and Arjun. He is involved with UNICEF and also owns restaurants in Mumbai and Bangalore.
